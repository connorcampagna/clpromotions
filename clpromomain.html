About Us

CL Promotions is a Glasgow-based music promotion company founded by Connor Campagna and Lewis Kirk. We're passionate about supporting emerging artists and bringing incredible live music experiences to Glasgow's vibrant music scene.

From venues like Room 2 to spaces like Stereo, we work with the best venues in the city to showcase talented artists and create unforgettable nights for those who love good music and supporting local venues.
